Frequently Asked Questions

We understand that learning to drive comes with many questions, and we're here to help! Below are some of the most common inquiries about our driving courses, lessons, and test preparation. If you don’t see your question here, feel free to contact us for more information.

Will you arrange for a driver's license for me

Yes, we start processing your license right from the day we present you to DVLA for your learner's license

What are the requirements to enroll in driving lessons?

To enroll, you typically need to meet the minimum age requirement for your region and hold a valid learner’s permit. Some programs may also require identification and basic vision screening before starting lessons.

How long does it take to complete a driving course

The duration varies based on the program and your learning pace. On average, a full course can take a few weeks to a couple of months, but express learning options are available for those who want to learn faster.

Do you offer both manual and automatic driving lessons?

Yes, we offer lessons for both manual and automatic vehicles. You can choose the option that best suits your needs and future driving preferences.

Can I choose my lesson schedule?

Absolutely! We offer flexible scheduling, including weekday, evening, and weekend classes, so you can learn at a time that fits your schedule.

What if I need extra lessons before taking my test?

If you feel you need more practice, we offer additional lessons to help you refine your skills and boost your confidence before your test.

How much do your driving lessons cost?

Prices vary depending on the type of course and number of lessons. Contact us for detailed pricing and package options to find the best fit for your needs.

What areas do you cover for driving lessons?

We provide lessons in various locations, covering both urban and suburban driving environments. Please reach out to confirm if we operate in your area.

Do you offer refresher courses for experienced drivers?

Yes, we offer refresher courses for those who want to improve their driving skills, regain confidence, or adapt to new driving conditions.

How soon can I book my driving test after completing the course?

This depends on test center availability, but we assist with scheduling your test as soon as you’re ready. We also provide mock tests to ensure you're well-prepared.

What happens if I fail my driving test?

If you don’t pass on your first attempt, don’t worry! We offer additional lessons and support to help you improve and retake the test with confidence.

Are your instructors certified and experienced?

Yes, all our instructors are fully certified, highly experienced, and committed to providing patient, professional, and effective driving instruction
